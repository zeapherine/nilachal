# Product Requirements Document (PRD): Nilachal Hospital & Research Centre

**Version**: 1.0  
**Status**: Draft  
**Owner**: Antigravity (Market Researcher & Web Analyst)  
**Project Goal**: Build a high-performance, professional, and mobile-responsive website to convert local search traffic into patients for Nilachal Hospital & Research Centre, Kokrajhar.

---

## 1. Executive Summary
Nilachal Hospital & Research Centre is a premier healthcare facility on **JD Road, Kokrajhar**, led by **Dr. S. C. Rabha**. With an inauguration set for **January 2026**, the website must establish a superior digital presence that outshines local competitors like MRM Hospital and RNB Civil Hospital by emphasizing modern infrastructure (NICU/ICU) and trust-based branding.

## 2. Target Audience
- **Local Residents**: Individuals in Kokrajhar seeking emergency care or specialized treatments.
- **Outstation Patients**: Patients from neighboring districts (Chirang, Bongaigaon) needing advanced surgical or neonatal facilities.
- **Hospital Administration**: To showcase Dr. Rabha's vision and professional standards.

## 3. Core Features & "Wow" Factors
### A. The "Emergency Header" (Mobile-First)
- **Sticky Overlay**: A high-visibility bar containing:
    - **Red CTA**: "Call Ambulance" (Direct Phone Trigger).
    - **Location Pin**: "Navigate to Hospital" (Deep link to Google Maps).
- **Requirement**: Must be persistently accessible on all pages.

### B. Specialized Department Modules
- Interactive, luxury-style cards for:
    - **Mother & Child Care**: Highlighting the state-of-the-art **NICU**.
    - **Critical Care**: Showcasing **ICU & Trauma** readiness.
    - **Advanced Surgery**: Featuring **Modular Operation Theatres (OT)**.
    - **24/7 Support**: Pharmacy, Diagnostics, and Emergency.

### C. Doctor Directory & Timings
- **Dynamic Grid**: Displaying Dr. S. C. Rabha and other specialists.
- **Metadata**: Photo, Designation, Qualification, and OPD timings.
- **CTA**: "Check Availability/Inquire" button.

### D. Communication & Conversion
- **WhatsApp Widget**: Sticky "Chat with Reception" (Bottom Right).
- **Appointment Form**: Simple 4-field capture (Name, Phone, Dept, Date) with immediate feedback simulation.
- **Language Toggle**: English/Bodo selector (Top Right) to cater to the local linguistic demographic.

## 4. Visual Identity & Sitemap
- **Color Palette**: 
  - Medical Blue (`#0056b3`)
  - Emergency Red (`#d9534f`)
  - Clean White (`#ffffff`)
- **Typography**: Inter / Roboto (Clean, Modern Sans-Serif).
- **Sitemap**:
  - **Home**: Hero image of the facility + "24/7 Open" Badge.
  - **About Us**: Focus on Dr. S. C. Rabha’s vision and the 2026 timeline.
  - **Departments**: Deep dive into medical services.
  - **Facilities**: Gallery section showing clean wards, ICU, and the Pharmacy.
  - **Contact**: Google Map embed + full address details.

## 5. Technical Specifications
- **Framework**: Next.js / Tailwind CSS for rapid, scalable, and responsive development.
- **Performance**: Targeted Google PageSpeed score of **90+** (Optimized for 4G).
- **SEO Strategy**:
  - Keywords: "Best Hospital in Kokrajhar", "NICU Kokrajhar", "Dr. S. C. Rabha Hospital".
  - Meta Description: Highlighting 24/7 emergency and advanced surgery.

---
## 6. Competitive Advantage
Unlike the functional but dated designs of local competitors, Nilachal's website will use **glassmorphism**, high-res clinical imagery, and smooth animations to communicate a "premium healthcare" experience.
